Dal Lake

Famous for Houseboats Shikara Rides Floating Gardens

Dal Lake is the jewel of Srinagar, famous for its houseboats and floating gardens. The lake covers an area of 18 square kilometers and is an integral part of tourism and recreation in Kashmir.

Gulmarg

Skiing Paradise Gondola Ride Golf Course

Gulmarg is known for its snow-capped peaks and world-class skiing facilities. It hosts one of the highest gondola rides in the world and is a popular destination for winter sports.

Pahalgam

Valley of Shepherds River Rafting Betaab Valley

Pahalgam, the Valley of Shepherds, is known for its scenic beauty, river rafting, and trekking routes. It serves as a base camp for Amarnath Yatra and offers various adventure activities.

Sonmarg

Meadow of Gold Thajiwas Glacier Trekking Routes

Sonmarg, meaning "Meadow of Gold," is known for its stunning landscapes, glaciers, and alpine flowers. It's a gateway to Ladakh and offers numerous trekking opportunities.

Kashmir Travel Information

The best time to visit Kashmir is from March to October when the weather is pleasant. However, for snow activities like skiing, December to February is ideal.

It depends on the season. For spring and summer, pack light clothing, comfortable shoes, sunglasses, and sunscreen. For winter, carry warm clothing, boots, and thermal wear.
